Elara succeeded in unraveling the Chronomancer’s Cipher, sealing the fractured timelines and restoring balance to the flow of time. The sanctum’s collapsing echoes stilled, and the world beyond the tower breathed again as if waking from a long, uneasy dream. Yet the victory came at a cost she had not foreseen—time itself demanded repayment.

As the final seal closed, Elara felt her memories begin to fade, one by one, slipping away like sand through open fingers. Faces she loved grew distant, their names dissolving into silence, until even her own story became uncertain. She returned to Elderglen as a stranger, a quiet wanderer drawn to a place she could no longer remember saving. The world was safe, whole, and healing—but Elara belonged to it no more, existing only in the faintest whispers of history she once protected.
